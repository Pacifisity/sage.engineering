# Chapter 59 - Exile Return

Sera returned on the third rain with warden scouts in the tannery drains.

They hit during shift change, when clinic security was thinnest and lantern sightlines were poor. First contact happened in the lower surgery corridor. By the time alarms carried upstairs, six people were already down.

Close quarters turned the cellar into a knife fight over cots and medicine carts.

When we pushed the attackers back, they retreated into the limestone processing room where quicklime bins lined the walls. Five dropped weapons and surrendered. Two kept fighting and were cut down.

I ordered the doors sealed.

No tribunal. No witness rail. No measured sentence.

We flooded the chamber with quicklime and pipe water.

The screaming lasted until the steam did.

Nara reached the corridor in time to see white slurry leaking under the threshold.

"Where is Sera?" she asked.

I did not answer.

She looked at the door and understood.

"You could have tried them," she said.

She walked past me to triage and did not speak to me again that night.

By dawn we held the clinic and counted cost: six medics dead, three patients dead, two orderlies missing.

In public report I wrote: TANNERY BREACH CONTAINED.

In private ledger I wrote what mattered:

Nara now looks at me as if my body is still here but the person is not.
