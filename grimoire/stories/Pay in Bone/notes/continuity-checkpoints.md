# Continuity Checkpoints

- Ch 1-10: Betrayal, exile, first kills, first Black Weight symptoms.
- Ch 11-20: New allies, city tax backlash, district scarred by Cael's rites.
- Ch 21-30: Failed mercy attempts, escalation of sacrifice logic.
- Ch 31-40: Warlock network forms; Cael reshapes hierarchy from below.
- Ch 41-50: Family retaliation intensifies; Cael answers with mass ritual.
- Ch 51-60: Territorial governance arc; Cael becomes feared administrator.
- Ch 61-70: Identity erosion arc; memory costs challenge motivation.
- Ch 71-80: Expanded campaign beyond home city; catastrophe powers growth.
- Ch 81-90: Near-confrontation and reversals; Sereth's control destabilizes.
- Ch 91-100: Open-ended dominion struggle; no final ending, only widening stakes.
