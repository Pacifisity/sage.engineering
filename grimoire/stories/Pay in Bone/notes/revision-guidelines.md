# Revision Guidelines (Active)

These guidelines are sourced from notes/review.md and apply to all chapter revision passes.

## Language
- Prefer modern, direct wording.
- Keep world-specific terms (ashmarks, sigils, vows, wardens) when needed.
- Remove archaic or ornamental phrasing that slows readability.

## Relationships
- Clarify Cael's dynamic with Yarrow through specific scenes, not labels.
- Clarify Cael's dynamic with Nara through practical conflict and care beats.
- Keep mystery, but do not hide core emotional logic.

## Internal Voice
- Reduce explicit foreshadowing and prophecy-like internal narration.
- Let characters make limited, present-tense decisions with imperfect information.

## Idioms
- Use idioms sparingly and intentionally.
- Reserve idioms for turning points, not routine narration.

## Continuity Safety
- Keep Cael as sole POV.
- Preserve major plot beats and consequences.
- Preserve established world mechanics and political structure.
