# Continuation Plan

This file now plans continuation from Chapter 16 onward.

## Primary Objective
Build from existing canon without retcon drift. Prioritize consequence chains over plot speed.

## Scope Lock For This Continuation Pass
- Narrative perspective remains single-MC only.
- Current active MC is Cael through his terminal arc.
- Post-Cael transition MC is Kess, his daughter and second MC.
- At Cael's death, Black Weight harvests Cael's cores and full memory architecture.
- Black Weight then rewards harvested cores and memory to Kess in exchange for attachment.

## Time and Geopolitical Locks
- Sereth's civil-uprising attrition war lasts 3 years before Cael's sacrifice phase.
- Kess is 3 years old at the time of Cael's sacrifice.
- Nara relocates to a new city under a different House after leaving.
- The city formerly under House Veyr control is held by Yarrow by post-attrition transition.

## Phase 1: Chapters 16-20 (Frontline Attrition War)
- Keep Cael physically on active fronts during civil uprising operations.
- Run Sereth's attrition pattern as block-by-block pressure, not remote legal play.
- Let Yarrow stabilize immediate post-Nara system failures (clinic triage, routes, dispatch discipline).
- End the block with Cael defeating Sereth in-field and Matriarch Ys harvesting Sereth's Origin.
- Stretch this conflict as the opening window of a 3-year war, not a short campaign.

## Phase 2: Chapters 21-25 (Predatory Consolidation)
- Show Matriarch Ys escalating from hidden manipulator to overt apex threat.
- Stage Drov's heart-wrenching betrayal: long service rewarded with consumption.
- Demonstrate post-absorption power asymmetry (high-order effects at minimal visible cost).
- Force Cael toward a sacrificial endgame option he cannot survive as a whole person.

## Phase 3: Chapters 26-30 (Sacrifice and Inheritance)
- Execute Cael's terminal sacrifice to end Matriarch Ys's two-Origin supremacy.
- Keep consequence absolute: Cael dies; identity continuity is broken.
- Black Weight harvests Cael's cores (gathered Origins) and complete memory architecture at the moment of death.
- Black Weight "rewards" both cores and memories to Kess in exchange for Kess accepting Black Weight's attachment; an active transaction, not burden inheritance.
- Kess inherits full harvested memory architecture plus actual power(s), creating a successor with resource-level stake in the relationship rather than psychological burden alone.
- Hand resistance leadership to Yarrow, whose surreal intelligence and coldness become structural.

## Balanced Arc Length Recommendation
- Recommended span: 22 chapters from current point to full transition.
- Fast floor: 18 chapters if compression is required.
- Preferred pacing uses 2,000+ words per chapter with room for aftermath chapters.
