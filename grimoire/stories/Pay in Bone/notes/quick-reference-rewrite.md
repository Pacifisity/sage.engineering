## MAGIC COST REFERENCE

### Blood-Fire (Ch 2+)
- **Cost per minute:** 1 year of life
- **Max duration:** ~5-10 minutes before unconsciousness
- **Signature:** Red flame, tastes like iron
- **Status Ch 12:** Cael has probably used ~15-20 minutes total; down 15-20 years of lifespan

### Bell-Casting (Ch 8+)
- **1 ring:** ~1 hour memory lost, range ~1 district
- **2 rings:** ~1 day memory lost, range ~3 districts
- **3 rings:** ~1 week memory lost, range entire city
- **Cost pattern:** Exponential, not linear
- **Status Ch 12:** Cael has cast 5-7 times total; lost roughly 1-2 months of continuous memory though not sequential

### Scar Lattice (Ch 9+)
- **Cost per cast:** Chronic pain + hemorrhage risk
- **Physical limit:** Can add max 8-10 sigils before cascade failure
- **Pain channeling:** More pain available = more raw power unlocked
- **Status Ch 12:** Cael has ~6 major sigils; one arm nerve-damaged from overuse

### Vow Magic (Ch 9+)
- **Vow:** "Never kneel to House Veyr"
- **Consequence if broken:** Body suffers joint pain, reduced casting precision, possible paralysis in fight
- **Binding strength:** Proportional to seriousness of oath intent
- **Status Ch 12:** Vow holds; Cael has not broken it (though Uncle Drov pressured)

### Channel Lifespan Buffer
- **Starting amount:** ~80-90 years (age 17-18 start)
- **Depletion tracking:** Blood-fire and emergency escalations drain it
- **Status Ch 12:** Approximately 65-70 years remaining; could feel it in endurance

## CHECKLIST: DOES THIS CHAPTER FEEL "LIVED IN"?

Before marking a chapter complete, ask:

- [ ] Does the reader understand what the protagonist *chose*, not just what happened?
- [ ] Are specific details given (prices, names, sensory moments) not just summary?
- [ ] Do consequences cascade into later chapters, or do they disappear?
- [ ] Is there at least one moment where the protagonist sits with the weight of their choice?
- [ ] Do relationships shift incrementally, not suddenly?
- [ ] Is the tone consistent with the story's grimdark foundation?
- [ ] Does the prose maintain quality (sensory, metaphorical, rhythmic)?
- [ ] Can the reader locate themselves in the physical space and emotional moment?
- [ ] Is there evidence of time passing (scars accumulate, prices change, people age)?
- [ ] Does the magic cost feel *real*, not just flavor?

If any answer is "no," expand that chapter before moving to the next.

## RED FLAGS (Stop & Rewrite If You See These)

🚩 **Summary Language:** "Cael did X and then Y" vs. "Cael chose X. The cost arrived as Y."
🚩 **Unmotivated Character Shift:** Yarrow goes from scout to killer instantly, not incrementally
🚩 **Consequence Vanishing:** Bridge collapses, then next scene ignores famine
🚩 **Generic Prose:** No specific sensory details; could be any grimdark story
🚩 **Sereth as Plot Device:** Appears only when needed for conflict; no independent agency
🚩 **Black Weight Forgotten:** Ch 11-12 doesn't reference it as ambient presence
🚩 **Nara's Exit Abrupt:** She quits without emotional weight or relationship history
🚩 **Magic Costs Unclear:** Reader can't calculate what price Cael paid
🚩 **Cael Emotionless:** No moment of sitting with guilt, moral failure, or cost
🚩 **Nara Not Pregnant:** If pregnant detail isn't planted by Ch 10, exit won't land
