# Character Notes

## Protagonist
- Cael Veyr: former heir-marked blood warlock whose Origin Sigil was stolen by family.
- Moral trajectory: antihero to villain-leaning strategist willing to kill and sacrifice others.
- Psychological core: betrayal fixation, sleep deprivation, survivor guilt, ritual dependency.

## Family Betrayal Arc
- Sereth Veyr (older sister): stole Cael's Origin Sigil and transferred it into her own marrow using forbidden kin rites.
- Matron Ys Veyr (mother): sanctioned the theft to preserve family rank.
- Uncle Drov Veyr: arranged legal cover and bounty framing.

## Major Supporting Cast
- Nara Quill: debt surgeon and sigil mender; practical, compassionate, compromised.
- Brother Halvek: defrocked war-priest who teaches vow-binding and tactical cruelty.
- Irix Thorn: assassin-poet from the Guild Chains; opportunistic ally.
- Warden-Captain Morn Hal: regime enforcer who admires Cael's efficiency.
- Tamsin Rook: smuggler queen who trades safe passage for bodies and secrets.
- Yarrow Pike: child scavenger and witness to Cael's worst choices.
- Lio Ferr: archivist obsessed with documenting apocalypse rituals.
- Sereth's Hand, Karel Dune: elite hunter tasked with ending Cael.

## Ongoing Trauma Markers
- Cael loses smell in one nostril after a blood-fire ritual.
- Cael cannot remember his father's final words after trading that memory.
- Cael keeps a ledger of names sacrificed, then repeatedly burns and rewrites it.
