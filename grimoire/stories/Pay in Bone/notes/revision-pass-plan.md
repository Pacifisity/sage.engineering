# Pay in Bone Revision Pass Plan

## Goal
Revise prose quality without changing core plot architecture.

## Source Notes To Apply
- Modernize language where possible: remove unnecessary archaic terms.
- Deepen relationship clarity, especially Cael-Nara and Cael-Yarrow dynamics.
- Reduce direct foreshadowing from internal monologue.
- Use idioms sparingly and with weight.

## Pass Structure
1. Pass A - Voice and readability (chapters 1-25)
2. Pass B - Relationship depth and clarity (chapters 26-50)
3. Pass C - Stakes continuity and emotional arc coherence (chapters 51-75)
4. Pass D - Endgame tightening and payoff consistency (chapters 76-100)
5. Pass E - Whole-book polish (cadence, repeated phrasing, final consistency)

## Revision Rules
- Keep Cael as sole POV.
- Preserve grimdark tone, but reduce overwritten phrasing.
- Keep world terms that are core setting vocabulary.
- Replace ornamental archaic wording with direct modern phrasing.
- Ensure each chapter has at least one concrete interpersonal beat.
- Keep ending costs explicit and distinct per chapter.

## Acceptance Criteria
- No obvious copy-loop leftovers.
- No chapter-end boilerplate artifacts.
- Relationship beats are readable without external notes.
- Idioms appear as emphasis, not filler.
- Continuity remains aligned with notes/continuity-ledger.md.

## Execution Order
- Revise in 10-chapter blocks.
- After each block, update notes/revision-status.md.
- After each block, spot-check 2 chapters for continuity drift.
