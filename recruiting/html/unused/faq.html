Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join with tattoos?

Many tattoos are allowed, but placement and content matter. Policies change, so an official recruiter can confirm current eligibility.

What ASVAB score do I need?

Requirements vary by job (MOS). Higher scores open more options. I can share study resources if you want.

What is Basic Training like?

It is challenging but designed to build fitness, discipline, and teamwork. You will be supported by drills and peers.
