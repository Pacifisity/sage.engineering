Capabilities
[HackRF]
Established control over the drone; we are able to capture the RF (Radio Frequency) and replay it to disrupt and manipulate the drone operation over the Key Bridge.
[Arduino]
By intercepting the signals, we can modify the directions sent to the drone in order to disrupt the effectiveness of the drones. By using our transmitter, we can send RF signals and take control of the drone or disrupt current signals.
[Reversing]
We are able to capture live, raw flight data within Donovian airspace and write that data to a file. To replay the attack, we are using netcat to pipe the raw data file into enemy system port 30001, which is the port that dump1090 uses for input. This creates false air traffic on their system.
