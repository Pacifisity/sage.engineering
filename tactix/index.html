Tactix Game Guide: Tier Lists, Mutations, and Merging Strategies

Merging Rules

Merge Result
0★ to 1★ 75% of the base power of the sum of both units
1★ to 2★ 100% of the base power of the sum of both units
2★ to 3★ 100% of the base power of the sum of both units

Tier List

  • Meta: Fire, Electric
  • Good: Wind
  • Okay: Water, Grass, Ice
  • Terrible: Gold

Mutations

Type Price Effect
Gold 2x No known effect
Ice 2x Slows enemy, +10% ATK
Wind 3x Increased unit movement speed
Water 4x Gives shield for double the health
Grass 5x Each hit gives HP to unit
Fire 6x 20% AoE attack, +20% ATK
Electric 6x 20% chain attack

Advanced Mutations

Multi-mutation units are possible: Merging two mutated units has a 1% or 2% chance of giving the element to the unit.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tactix

What is the best mutation in Tactix?

Fire and Electric are considered the best due to their high attack and chain potential.

How do I merge units effectively?

Use the merging rules depending on star level: 0★→1★ = 75%, 1★→2★ = 100%, 2★→3★ = 100% of combined unit power.
