Tactix Game Guide

This guide covers unit tier rankings, mutation effects, and merging mechanics to help you make informed decisions while playing Tactix.

Merging Rules

Merge Tier Resulting Power
0★ → 1★ 75% of the combined base power of both units
1★ → 2★ 75% of the combined base power of both units
2★ → 3★ 75% of the combined base power of both units

Tier List

  • Meta: Fire, Electric
  • Strong: Wind
  • Average: Water, Grass, Ice
  • Weak: Gold

Mutations

Mutation Type Cost Multiplier Effect
Gold 2× No confirmed effect
Ice 2× Slows enemies and increases ATK by 10%
Wind 3× Increases unit movement speed
Water 4× Grants a shield equal to twice the unit’s health
Grass 5× Each hit restores HP to the unit
Fire 6× 20% AoE damage and +20% ATK
Electric 6× 20% chance to chain attacks to additional targets

Advanced Mutations

Multiple mutations on a single unit are possible. When merging two mutated units, there is a 1–2% chance for the resulting unit to inherit one of the mutations. The same mutation cannot be applied more than once. A unit may have up to 7 different mutations, one of each type.
