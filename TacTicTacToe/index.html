How to Play

  • 1. The game consists of 9 mini tic-tac-toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid.
  • 2. Your move in a mini-board determines which mini-board your opponent must play in next (highlighted in purple).
  • 3. If you're sent to a completed mini-board, you can play in any available mini-board.
  • 4. Win three mini-boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win the game!
